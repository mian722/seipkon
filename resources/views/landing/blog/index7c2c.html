Offerslook Announces New Cost-reducing Pricing Model Option to Customers

May 31, 2017 By Offerslook

Offerslook, one of the leading performance marketing Software Company today announces new Pricing Option – Pay for conversions, to customers. The conversion-based model will significantly reduce the cost of the networks.

Offerslook CEO Myron said: As the market is more and more focus on performance, charge by clicks is becoming a burden for many of our customers. Our clients need an efficient and less expensive way to freely deal with their digital marketing campaigns.

Compare to charge by clicks, for a networks with a monthly clicks around 100 Million，the new pricing model can potentially lower the price by 50 percent.

 

Start-Up Enterprise Custom
$259/ Month $459/ Month $Custom
10,000 Conversions Included 40,000 Conversions Included ＞ 40,000 Conversions
$0.018 per additional conversion $0.015 per additional conversion Special offer

 

Additionally, considering some of the customer’s needs, Offerslook still keep the click –based pricing with a Pay as you go model.

For the detailed pricing information, please refer to https://www.offerslook.com/pricing.html
