A Preview of Offerslook Upcoming New Features

Aug 15, 2017 By Offerslook

 

Offerslook Team will attend DMEXCO 2017, crucial worldwide digital event, to be held in September 13–14 at Cologne, Germany.

During the exposition, Offerslook CEO and top Business Development Managers will show you how to increase your performance marketing ROI and efficiency in an easy and smart way with Offerslook upcoming new features:

Smart Puller
With Offerslook Smart Puller, you can quickly and automatically grab as many offers and related information from other systems into your network by a few clicks. This automated function enable you to enjoy a free way to transfer your offers and no need to worry any errors and boring manually routine work. It will support these networks and platforms including Cake, HasOffers, Fuseclikcs and many others.

The most important, it will be completely free for your use.

SmartLink
Let Offerslook SmartLink auto-optimize your traffic. With Offerslook SmartLink, you only need to create one link which will auto-detect the user’s info and dynamically redirect users to the best offers that could maximize your ROI without sacrificing user experience.

Email Marketing
When it comes to run an ad/affiliate network, communication with advertisers and affiliates always accounts for a big part of daily work. With Offerslook powerful Email Marketing, you can create and send emails with texts, pics or HTML files to any desired audiences, the affiliates, advertisers and employees. That being said, networks on Offerslook will be able to promote offers among all affiliates!

Smart Alert
Offerslook Smart Alert was designed to alert and handle the abnormal issues in real time during your business process. Instead of constantly logging in to check the progress of your campaigns, Offerslook Smart Alert can notify you proactively of things you care about. It supports a variety of metrics to alert on and provide deep insights into the health and performance of your Ads campaigns. You can receive alerts for key aspects of your campaigns to ensure they’re meeting your benchmarks.
