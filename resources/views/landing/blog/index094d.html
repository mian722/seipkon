Big-Bang Features Update: Offer Redirect & Cap Alerting

Dec 12, 2016 By Offerslook

Marketers strive to make sure each click counts and reduce waste of traffic, and Offerslook is dedicated to empowering customers make full use of each click. Two big features released today will let clicks generate more value and let you optimize traffic — Offer Redirect & Cap Alerting.

Offer Redirect

Normally when the traffic does not meet the targeting settings, e.g. the offer is expired, caps exceeded, or geo-targeting is not matched, these traffic will be not be directed to appropriate page. However with Offer Redirect, these traffic can be optimized and sent to the right offers.

Offerslook now provides you triple back-up solutions — Redirect Offer, Offer Pools and Global Redirect — Traffic from a user can be redirected maximum 3 times, which covers 98% of the chances traffic reaching an offer. We ensure that you have the highest possibility to make best use of your traffic and increase your profits.

Check out how to set Redirect Offer, Offer Pools and Global Redirect and get the most value from your traffic.

Cap Alerting

We had previously given tutorials on how to set caps for offers, and how to set offer caps for affiliates, so that you manage to give a budget limit for certain offers. However, your affiliates can be confused if they have no idea whether these caps are consumed.

Cap alerting, for both Offer Caps and Affiliate Caps, is the answer to letting affiliates drive traffic more wisely. The alert settings for Offer Caps and Affiliate Caps can help you make timely and better decisions for offers with limited quota. And these specific alerts include Offer Caps Approaching, Offer Caps Reached, Affiliate Caps Approaching, and Affiliate Caps Reached.

With proper setting, the system can automatically push emails or in-platform notifications when the cap limit is almost reached or full, helping you to save time in notifying your affiliates one by one and make advanced arrangements. Also for your affiliates, this will largely help them to save redundant traffic and avoid wasting traffic.

Check out this tutorial — Alert Settings for Offer Caps & Affiliate Caps.
