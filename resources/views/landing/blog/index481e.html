Brand New Reporting System

May 11, 2018 By Offerslook

To enhance our clients’ reporting experiences, Offerslook updated the reporting system and made it more convenient for you to check from any reporting dimension you want to measure, analyze, and optimize on the data so as to drive higher performance.

Consider the working scene, we seperate the general report into three parts.

  1. Advertiser report
  2. Affiliate report
  3. Operation report

 

Advertiser Report / Affiliate Report:

Enable the different roles in company to check the information that is most important for them .

For example, the affiliate manager can find out which affiliate are generating the most and highest quality traffic from the affiliate report directly while the BD person can check how well each advertiser’s offer on the network is converting from the advertiser report.

Using various filters, you can also create the custom report to meet your requirement, no need to check the boxes of the related information every time.

Operation Report:

 

The reports include: advertiser, offer, affiliate and smart link. Take the affiliate part for example:

Utilizing this report, you are able to check how each affiliates are performing. You might find that some certain affiliates are performing better than others and then you can look into the next level to check which offers are performing well under the good-performance affiliate and keep drill down to obtain more information such as: source ID, country, platform, mobile Carrier.

Equipped with this knowledge, you can now make a quick decision on how to optimize and focus your attention and traffic on the high performing offer and affiliates.

 

 

In a word, with this new reporting system, you can pull the information that is most important for you easily and directly to help you check the performance and make a future decision.

Furthermore, with the update of the reporting system, now you can experience a much more fast loading speed of the report.

 Dive into the reports today and take your performance to the next level! To learn more about each report and see how they work, please log into your account to experience or contact your account manager for more information and help.
